Welcome!

Coolstation is a classic-experience Goonstation fork Space Station 13 server. It aims to recreate a rougher, rowdier, juicier era of gameplay.
New to the game? View the Quickstart guide!
Our quickstart guide is INCREDIBLY INCOMPLETE so in the meantime consider the tutorial from our fork's originator: Goonstation!
Have questions about how to do things? Push F3 or use mentorhelp for answers.
Need an admin? Push F1 or use the adminhelp command.
If no admins are currently online, adminhelp queries get mirrored to Discord and will more than likely still be seen.
